Racket

Passive: Master Engineer - Racket controls a flying drone that autoattacks nearby enemies and responds to his abilites. The drone gains 50% of Rackets attack damage, health, magic resistance and armor, and attacks once every second.
When killed, a new drone will spawn after 30 seconds (lowered by cooldown reduction). However, Racket can collect the destroyed drone, reducing the cooldown of Master Engineer by 15 seconds (W, E and R are disabled if there is no active drone).gg

Q: Phenon Maser - 25 Mana - 3 seconds cooldown.
After a brief delay, shoots a beam of energy damaging all enemies in a straight line and dealing 100/140/180/220/260 (+0.8 ap) damage to the first target. Subsequent targets lower the damage by 15% each. The drone mimics this ability dealing 75% of its damage.

W: Shock Crash - 60/70/80/90/100 Mana - 20/18/16/14/12 seconds cooldown.
Commands the drone to crash into the ground dealing 80/125/170/215/250 (+0.3 ad) damage to nearby enemies. The drone then becomes invulnerable, stops moving, and generates a shocking field slowing enemies' movement and attack speed by 25/30/35/40/45% for 2 seconds.

E: Dimensional Reversal - 50/60/70/80/90 Mana - 14/13/12/11/10 seconds cooldown.
Racket and his drone switch places. Afterwards Racket gains 20/30/40/50/60% bonus attack speed for 3 seconds, the drone releases a psion pulse, pushing nearby enemies briefly away.

R: Barrier - 100 Mana - 60 seconds cooldown.
The drone immediately returns to Racket, and creates a magic barrier reducing damage taken by 25/30/35% for 6 seconds. Over the duration, the drone heals for 100% of her health and deals 50/100/150% more damage with autoattacks and spells.
